Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an exercise?

Answer

In order to create an exercise you must be logged in, and you can log in Here or if you don't have an account you can create one Here. Once you are logged in there will be an option to create exercise under the exercise heading at the top of the page.

Q: I made a mistake, how do I change the exercise I created?

Answer

In order to edit any exercise you create you go to the My Exercises page. From there you find the exercise you are looking for and click the green pencil button in the corner.

Q: Why can't I see the exercise I just created?

Answer

This is because each exercise must be approved by a moderator before other users may see it. You may still see it and edit it at the My Exercises page.

Q: Why can't I see my exercise after I edit it?

Answer

After you edit an exercise it has to undergo the same approval proccess that a new exercise must undergo.

Q: How do I become a Moderator?

Answer

Unfortunately at this time there is no way to become a Moderator.
