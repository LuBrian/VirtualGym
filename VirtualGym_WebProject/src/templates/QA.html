Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an exercise?

Answer

In order to create an exercise you must be logged in, and you can log in Here Once you are logged in there will be an option to create exercise under the exercise heading at the top of the page.

Q: I made a mistake, how do I change the exercise I created?

Answer

In order to edit any exercise you create you go to the Edit Exercises page. From there you find the exercise you are looking for and click the green pencil button in the corner.

Q: Why can't I see the exercise I just created?

Answer

This is because each exercise must be approved by a moderator before other users may see it. You may still see it and edit it at the Edit Exercises page.

Q: Why can't I see my exercise after I edit it?

Answer

After you edit an exercise it has to undergo the same approval proccess that a new exercise must has to.

Q: How do I become a Moderator?

Answer

Unfortunately at this time there is no way to become a Moderator.

Q: When creating an exercise, how do I place text on the video?

Answer

While you are creating or editing an exercise you may click on the video to add an annotation at that time and place during the video. If you make a mistake you can remove them by clicking the "x" on the annotation timestamp below the video.
